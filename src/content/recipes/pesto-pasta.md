## Components
- [Home made pesto](/recipes/home-made-pesto/)
- [Homemade pasta (fresh)](/recipes/homemade-pasta-fresh/)

## Assembly
1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water.
2. Loosen pesto with a splash of pasta water.
3. Toss pasta with pesto and serve immediately.
