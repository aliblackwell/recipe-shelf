## Ingredients
- Fresh ginger
- Rice vinegar
- Sugar
- Salt

## Steps
1. Slice ginger very thinly.
2. Heat vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
3. Pour over ginger and cool.
4. Chill before using.
