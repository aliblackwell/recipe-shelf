## Components
- [Flat bread (fresh)](/recipes/flat-bread/)
- [Home made hummus](/recipes/home-made-hummus/)
- [Home made falafel](/recipes/home-made-falafel/)
- Lettuce
- [Garlic sauce](/recipes/garlic-sauce/)
- [Chilli sauce](/recipes/chilli-sauce/)
- [Pickled cabbage](/recipes/pickled-cabbage/)

## Assembly
1. Warm flatbreads.
2. Spread hummus across each bread.
3. Add falafel, lettuce, and pickles.
4. Finish with garlic and chilli sauces.
5. Roll tight and serve.
