## Overview
A dense, nutty sourdough with seeds folded through and sprinkled on top.

## Ingredients
- Starter (active)
- Wholemeal flour
- Water
- Salt
- Seed mix (sunflower, pumpkin, flax, sesame)

## Steps
1. Mix starter, water, and flour.
2. Rest, then add salt and fold in seeds.
3. Fold every 30 minutes for 2 to 3 hours.
4. Shape, then cold-proof overnight.
5. Bake until the crust is dark and seeds are toasted.
