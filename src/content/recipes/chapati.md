## Overview
A no-frills chapati method you can run on repeat.

## Ingredients
- Wholemeal flour
- Water
- Salt

## Steps
1. Mix and knead to a soft dough.
2. Rest briefly.
3. Divide and roll evenly.
4. Cook both sides on a hot dry pan.
