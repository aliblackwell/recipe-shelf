## Overview
This is your base recipe slot for fresh pasta. Use this page as the canonical method as you refine ratios and shaping notes.

## Ingredients
- 00 flour
- Eggs
- Pinch of salt

## Method
1. Mix flour and eggs to form a dough.
2. Knead until smooth.
3. Rest 30 minutes.
4. Roll and cut.
