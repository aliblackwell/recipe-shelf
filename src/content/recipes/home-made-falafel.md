tags: ["prep", "falafel"]
---

## Ingredients
- Soaked chickpeas
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley or coriander
- Cumin
- Salt

## Steps
1. Pulse ingredients into a coarse mix.
2. Chill mix to firm up.
3. Shape into balls or patties.
4. Fry or air-fry until crisp.
