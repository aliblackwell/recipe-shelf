## Ingredients
- Salmon
- Rice
- Spring onions
- Sesame seeds
- [Pickled ginger](/recipes/pickled-ginger/)
- [Pickled cabbage](/recipes/pickled-cabbage/)

## Steps
1. Cook rice and let it cool slightly.
2. Slice salmon and spring onions.
3. Build bowls with rice and salmon.
4. Top with sesame seeds and pickles.
