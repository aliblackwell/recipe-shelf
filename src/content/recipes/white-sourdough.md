## Overview
A reliable white sourdough that fits a slow, overnight rhythm. Adjust hydration and folds as you dial it in.

## Ingredients
- Starter (active)
- Strong white bread flour
- Water
- Salt

## Steps
1. Mix starter, water, and flour until shaggy.
2. Rest 30 to 45 minutes, then add salt and mix to combine.
3. Fold every 30 minutes for 2 to 3 hours.
4. Shape, then cold-proof overnight.
5. Bake in a preheated Dutch oven or cloche until deep golden.

## Notes
- Track hydration and bake time here as you refine the loaf.
