## Overview
A high-hydration focaccia with room for your bread sauce flavor notes.

## Ingredients
- Strong white flour
- Water
- Yeast or starter
- Salt
- Olive oil

## Steps
1. Mix dough and rest until smooth.
2. Bulk ferment with a couple of folds.
3. Oil tray, dimple dough, and proof.
4. Bake until deeply golden.
