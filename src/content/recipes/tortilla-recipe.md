## Ingredients
For 8 tortillas:
- 500g flour
- 330g lukewarm water
- 10g salt
- 6 tablespoons olive oil

For 4 tortillas:
- 250g flour
- 165g lukewarm water
- 5g salt
- 3 tablespoons olive oil

## Method
1. Mix it all together.
2. Rest for thirty minutes.
3. Ball it up so the sides are smooth.
4. Divide into equal pieces.
5. Roll out on a floured surface.
6. Cook in a hot heavy-bottomed pan.

Enjoy.
