## Overview
A wet dough bread that rewards gentle handling.

## Ingredients
- Strong flour
- Water
- Yeast
- Salt
- Olive oil (optional)

## Steps
1. Mix to a sticky dough.
2. Fold periodically during bulk rise.
3. Divide and shape with minimal degassing.
4. Bake hot with steam until deeply colored.
