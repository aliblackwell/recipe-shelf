## Ingredients
- Cooked chickpeas
- Tahini
- Lemon juice
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Salt

## Steps
1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon, and garlic.
2. Add water or olive oil until smooth.
3. Season to taste.
