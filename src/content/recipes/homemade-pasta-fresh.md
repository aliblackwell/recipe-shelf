tags: ["prep", "pasta"]
---

## Ingredients
- 00 flour
- Eggs
- Pinch of salt

## Steps
1. Mix flour and eggs into a dough.
2. Knead until smooth and elastic.
3. Rest for 30 minutes.
4. Roll and cut to your preferred shape.
