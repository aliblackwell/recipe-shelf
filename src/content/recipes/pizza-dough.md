## Overview
Flexible dough for high-heat bakes at home.

## Ingredients
- Flour
- Water
- Salt
- Yeast
- Olive oil (optional)

## Steps
1. Mix and knead until elastic.
2. Bulk ferment until risen.
3. Divide into dough balls and rest.
4. Stretch, top, and bake hot.
