## Overview
Simple pan-cooked breads that pair with wraps and falafel.

## Ingredients
- Flour
- Water
- Salt
- Olive oil

## Steps
1. Mix into a soft dough.
2. Rest 20 to 30 minutes.
3. Divide and roll thin.
4. Cook in a hot pan until puffed and spotted.
