## Ingredients
- Basil
- Pine nuts or mixed nuts
- Parmesan
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Salt

## Steps
1. Blend basil, nuts, parmesan, and garlic.
2. Drizzle in olive oil to desired consistency.
3. Season and store chilled.
