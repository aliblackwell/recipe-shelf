---

## Ingredients
- Red cabbage
- Vinegar
- Water
- Sugar
- Salt

## Steps
1. Shred cabbage finely.
2. Heat pickling liquid until sugar dissolves.
3. Pour over cabbage and cool.
4. Chill before serving.
